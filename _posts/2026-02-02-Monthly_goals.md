# Qualifying Exam Studying
I have received some reading lists (primarily academic papers, and one book) and have been thinking about a study plan for a while. I am now starting to implement the process! 

I am using Zotero to read the papers and am highlighting according to a color coding system where I highlight Yellow: main ideas/ takeaways; Green: Definitions, methodology, technical terms; Pink: Important supporting details, evidence, statistics; Blue: citations or quotes - references that need to be followed up; Red: Controversial points, crucial questions, critical results. 

I am taking notes in Zotero and will be linking papers to connect broader themes (there's a way to link papers using notes in the Zotero app). 

I will soon also be setting aside writing practice time. I'll write another notebook post later this month detailing the study and preparation methods that seem to be working best as I go along. 

My qualifying exam is going to be March 16-20th. One question/day for 5 days.
