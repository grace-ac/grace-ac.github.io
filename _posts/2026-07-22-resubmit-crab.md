Wiley Molecular Ecology rejected our paper, but suggested it for submission to one of their other journals. See post for details.

Transfer options posed by Wiley Molecular Ecology were Wiley Ecology and Evolution; Wiley Journal of Fish Diseases; and Wiley Journal of Fish Biology. 

Steven and I chatted today and picked Journal of Fish Diseases: [The Journal of Fish Diseases is the medium for all aspects of disease in both wild and cultured fish and shellfish.](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/journal/13652761). 

I submitted it to the journal using the submission transfer option. Stay tuned!
