## Kit and protocol info: 

ZymoBIOMICS DNA MiniPrep Kit --> D4300 

Extraction protocol notes: [Protocol_eDNA-extraction_protocol_GAC_2026Samples](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1s0auROq_3DyB4Hh2Jaw2QkpfmojLRoo6oEsJdvEcBUI/edit?usp=sharing)

I used the Eppendorf Centrifuge 5424 in FTR 209 that holds 24 tubes.

I took up the protocol for the optional stopping point at Step 2 [Page 6](https://github.com/grace-ac/project-pycno-multispecies-2023/blob/main/protocols/FilterDNA_extraction_protocol_d4300t_d4300_d4304_zymobiomics_dna_miniprep_kit.pdf) to have a check-in meeting with Drew... but as a note, the stoppage point was pre-bead-beating, but post-lysis solution addition. Other than that, everything went smoothly and the DNA is in the same box as the other three batches in the -80C in FTR.
